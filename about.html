What is it all about?

Viscount Valentine is an interior design blog, offering insights into the working mind and personal opinions of an interior designer. It has old and new design knowledge, explains how areas of the industry work, visually explains the ongoing creative process, is given to on-trend analysis, and features guidance to make your home a stylish sanctuary. It's all done with style, panache, sordid humour, and is always true to opulent elegance...
